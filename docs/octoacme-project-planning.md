# OctoAcme — Project Planning

## Purpose
Turn an approved initiative into an actionable plan and backlog for delivery.

## Objectives
- Break work into shippable increments
- Identify dependencies and risks
- Align timelines, releases, and responsibilities

## Activities
1. Kickoff meeting with stakeholders and delivery team
2. Create prioritized backlog with acceptance criteria
3. Estimate scope (T-shirt sizing or story points)
4. Agree on Definition of Ready and Definition of Done
5. Identify dependencies and integration points
6. Populate RAID log with initial risks, assumptions, and dependencies
7. Create release plan and milestone map

## Sprint / Iteration Planning
- Timebox planning to agreed sprint length
- Pull items that meet the [Definition of Ready](templates/definition-of-ready-and-done.md)
- Ensure team capacity is respected

## Risk & Dependency Management
- Capture all risks, assumptions, issues, and dependencies in the [RAID Log](templates/raid-log-template.md)
- For detailed risk tracking (scoring, escalation SLAs), use the [Risk Register Template](templates/risk-register-template.md)
- Mark cross-team dependencies in the project board and escalate during weekly syncs

## Planning Checklist
- [ ] Project kickoff held
- [ ] Backlog prioritized and estimated
- [ ] Release timeline and milestones agreed
- [ ] [Definition of Ready & Done](templates/definition-of-ready-and-done.md) documented and agreed by team
- [ ] [RAID Log](templates/raid-log-template.md) populated with initial risks, assumptions, and dependencies
- [ ] Initial test plan / QA approach drafted
- [ ] [RACI Matrix](templates/raci-matrix-template.md) reviewed and confirmed
